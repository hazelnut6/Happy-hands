4-Steps Enrollment Process

  1. ADMISSION APPLICATION ~ happyhandsacademy.edu.ph
  2. PRE-ENROLLMENT ~ email notification regarding the online system log-in details. Viewing of Enrollment Assessment Fees(EAF) in the student portal.
  3. PAYMENT ~ payment of at least the minimum down payment in any affiliated banks.
  4. ENROLLMENT CONFIRMATION ~ email Notification of enrollment confirmation. Issuance of official Student Sign in Form through the student online portal.
